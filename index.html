Hi! I'm a Geospatial Data Scientist specializing in GIS, Remote Sensing, and Spatial Data Science.

Seasoned Geospatial Data Scientist with over 16 years of expertise in GIS and remote sensing service development, specializing in automation of GIS and remote sensing workflows, geospatial risk analysis, GIS software development for climatic and environmental monitoring applications. I possess extensive international experience supporting countries across Asia, the Middle East, Europe, the Caribbean, and Africa with international organizations such as UNICEF, UN-WFP, NASA, the European Union, African Union.

Skills and expertise

Areas of Expertise

  • Innovative in software development tailored to geospatial analysis requirements.
  • Expert in computer programming for GIS applications and automating geospatial workflows utilizing Python, GDAL, ArcPy, Linux, and R, enhancing efficiency and accuracy.
  • Skilled in Geospatial Cloud Computing with Google Earth Engine (GEE) and adept in version control with Git.
  • Advanced in Data Science and Analytics, utilizing tools such as Power BI, Tableau, and R Shiny, coupled with expertise in mobile data collection via ODK.

  • Development of GIS and remote sensing products for parametric insurance.
  • Geodatabase development and management (Postgres, PostGIS, and Spatialite).
